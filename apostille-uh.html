If you're a UH graduate or current international student needing an apostille, the process involves three parties: UH's registrar, the Texas Secretary of State, and our office coordinating between them.

The Process

How UH Apostille Works

Step 01
Order Sealed Documents from UH

Request a sealed official transcript or certified diploma copy from UH's registrar. The registrar's seal must remain unbroken — do not open the envelope.

Step 02
Send to Our Cibolo Office

Mail the sealed documents to Texas Notary Kristy in Cibolo, TX, or have UH ship directly to us. We confirm receipt and intake within one business day.

Step 03
SOS Apostille Coordination

We submit your documents to the Texas Secretary of State in Austin for apostille certification. Standard processing is 5-10 business days; expedited options available.

Step 04
Domestic or International Shipping

Apostilled documents are shipped via DHL or USPS to your final destination. International shipping fully coordinated — tracked, insured, and customs-ready.
