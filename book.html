Before Your Appointment

How to Prepare

Do Not Pre-Sign

Documents must not be signed prior to the appointment. All signatures must be executed in the notary's presence to be valid.

Valid Identification

Bring a current, unexpired government-issued photo identification — driver's license, U.S. passport, or military ID card.

Witness Coordination

If your document requires witnesses, arrange for them in advance. Contact us prior to the appointment to coordinate if needed.

RON Checklist

For remote sessions: stable internet, functioning camera and microphone, government ID, and documents uploaded before the session begins.
