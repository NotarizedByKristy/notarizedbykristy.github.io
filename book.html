Before Your Appointment
How to Prepare
📋 Don't Sign Early

Do not sign your documents before the appointment. Signatures must be made in the notary's presence to be valid.

🪪 Bring Your ID

Bring a valid, government-issued photo ID — driver's license, passport, or military ID. It must be current and unexpired.

👥 Arrange Witnesses

If your document requires witnesses (wills, some POAs), arrange them in advance or contact us — we can help coordinate.

💻 RON Checklist

For RON sessions: stable internet, working camera and microphone, government ID, and documents uploaded before your session.
