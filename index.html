About Kristy

Veteran. Notary.
Business Owner.

Kristy is a retired U.S. Navy veteran with 21+ years of service, a Texas-commissioned notary public, NNA Certified Signing Agent, and the founder of Notarized By Kristy LLC — a mobile notary and document services business based in Cibolo, TX.

She holds SDVOSB, VOSB, and WOSB federal certifications, is SAM.gov registered, and serves clients ranging from individual borrowers to title companies, law firms, and government agencies.
