About Kristy Davis

Veteran. Notary.
Business Owner.

Kristy Davis is a retired United States Navy veteran with more than 21 years of service, a Texas-commissioned notary public, and NNA Certified Notary Signing Agent. She founded Texas Notary Kristy — a DBA of Notarized By Kristy LLC — serving the San Antonio metropolitan area from Cibolo, Texas.

Her practice serves individual clients, title companies, law firms, mortgage lenders, and government agencies — statewide via Remote Online Notarization and across Bexar, Guadalupe, and Comal counties for mobile engagements.

She holds Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB), and Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) federal certifications, and is registered in SAM.gov for government contracting.
