University Apostille Service

Coordinated Apostille for Texas University Documents

If you graduated from a Texas university and now need your diploma, transcript, or academic certifications authenticated for use abroad — for foreign employment, immigration, professional licensing, or graduate school — the Texas Secretary of State apostille is the document that makes that happen. We coordinate the entire process: from receiving sealed documents from your university's registrar, to SOS submission and certification in Austin, to final delivery anywhere in the world.

Our service is built for both international students and graduates navigating their own apostille needs, and for university registrars and International Student Services offices looking for a trusted referral partner for their students.

When You Need an Apostille

Common Use Cases

Foreign Employment

Many international employers require apostilled diplomas to verify your degree before hiring — especially in finance, engineering, healthcare, and education sectors abroad.

Graduate School Abroad

Foreign graduate programs typically require apostilled transcripts as part of admission requirements. Master's, Ph.D., and professional school applications.

Professional Licensing

Foreign professional licensing boards (medicine, law, engineering, accounting) require apostilled academic credentials to verify educational qualifications.

Visa & Immigration

Many countries require apostilled academic documents as part of work visa, residency, or family-sponsored immigration applications.

The Process

How University Apostille Works

Step 01
Order Sealed Documents

Request a sealed official transcript or certified diploma copy from your university's registrar. The seal must remain unbroken for SOS apostille acceptance.

Step 02
Send to Our Cibolo Office

Mail the sealed documents to our Cibolo, TX office, or have your university ship directly to us. We confirm receipt within one business day.

Step 03
SOS Apostille Coordination

We submit your documents to the Texas Secretary of State in Austin. Standard processing is 5-10 business days; expedited options available.

Step 04
International Delivery

Apostilled documents are shipped via DHL or USPS to your destination. Tracking, insurance, and customs paperwork included for international.
