How We Work With Title Companies

Process & delivery standard.

What you should expect from intake through underwriter delivery. We commit to these standards in writing for every partnership engagement.

01 Assignment
Confirmed — 4 Business Hours

Assignment received via your preferred platform or email. Confirmation back to escrow within four business hours including time block, signer contact status, and any flagged document issues.

02 Pre-Signing
Document Review Completed

Full document review before arrival or RON start. Flagged inconsistencies, missing pages, or signer issues communicated to escrow before signing begins. No surprises at the closing table.

03 Signing
Snapshot to Escrow — 30 min

Closing executed on schedule. Snapshot or PDF confirmation of signed package emailed to escrow officer within 30 minutes of signing close. Witness coordination handled in-stride for Texas signings.

04 Delivery
Originals Shipped — Same Day

Original signed package shipped via overnight carrier same business day, with tracking number provided to escrow. RON packages delivered digitally same session. Itemized invoice within one business day.
