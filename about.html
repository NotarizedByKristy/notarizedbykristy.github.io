About Kristy

Retired U.S. Navy veteran. Texas-commissioned notary. Business owner. 21+ years of service — and the same standard applied to every appointment.

The Story

Service Was Never
Just a Job

Kristy Davis served 21+ years in the United States Navy before retiring and building Notarized By Kristy LLC from the ground up in Cibolo, Texas. The transition from military service to business ownership wasn't a departure from discipline — it was a direct application of it.

Every appointment reflects the same standard: show up prepared, execute accurately, communicate clearly, and never cut corners. That's not a mission statement. It's how the work gets done.

With over 2,000 completed notarizations, Notarized By Kristy LLC serves individual clients, law firms, title companies, lenders, and government agencies across the San Antonio metro and statewide via Remote Online Notarization.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

We offer general notary work, loan signings, estate planning documents, Remote Online Notarization (RON), mobile notary, apostille coordination, I-9 verification, healthcare notary, and specialty services including correctional facility visits and structured settlements.
We provide mobile notary services across Bexar, Guadalupe, and Comal counties — including San Antonio, Cibolo, Schertz, Universal City, Converse, New Braunfels, and Seguin. Extended travel is available by arrangement. RON is available statewide and nationwide.
You can book online through the Book Now page, call 210-628-9991, or email info@notarizedbykristy.com. Same-day appointments are available when scheduling permits. After booking you'll receive a confirmation with preparation instructions.
Bring a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or military ID) and the documents that need to be notarized. Do not sign the documents beforehand — signatures must be made in the notary's presence. If witnesses are required, arrange for them in advance or contact us to coordinate.
You need a reliable internet connection, a device with a camera and microphone (smartphone, tablet, or computer), a government-issued ID, and your documents uploaded to the platform in advance. We'll send you a link with instructions before your session.
Appointments are available Monday through Saturday with flexible hours including evenings. Contact us for same-day availability. We work around your schedule — not a 9-to-5 window.
Texas notary fees are $10 per notarized signature. Additional fees apply for travel, RON sessions, and specialized services. All pricing is transparent and provided before your appointment — no surprises.
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and electronic payments including PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.
Appointments are time-reserved professional services. Cancellations or reschedules made within 2 hours of the scheduled appointment may result in applicable service or travel fees. No-shows are subject to the full reserved service fee.
