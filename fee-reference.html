Texas · Confirmed
$15 / doc
Texas SOS apostille fee, confirmed on the official SOS website. Walk-in same-day service available in Austin. Adoption docs: $10/doc, capped at $100 per child.
Texas Notary · Statutory Max
$10 / signature
Texas statutory notary fee: $10 for the first signature, $1 for each additional signature on the same document. Source: National Notary Association, 2026.
National Range
$1 – $25
Apostille fees range from $1 (Michigan) to $25 (New Jersey). Notary per-act maximums range from $2 (New York) to $25 (Rhode Island). Ten states have no statutory fee cap.
